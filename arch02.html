Village Upon a City

The project wins Virginia Tech Integrative Design in 2018. The construction site is in Chicago, the city of skyscrapers. A major problem identified in such an urban enviornment is an isolation between functional public and private areas. To obscure the spatial boundary of "inside" and "outside", I designed the program with layering spaces to create a delayed transition from the public environment, to the more private interior space of many of the buildings within the site.
